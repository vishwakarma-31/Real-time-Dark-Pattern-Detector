# DarkScan Extension Privacy Policy

## What data we collect

- URLs of pages analyzed.
- DOM snapshot text used for analysis (for example button labels, checkbox labels, and timer text).

We do **not** collect passwords, form inputs, personal data, or payment details.

## Where data is sent

Anonymized page analysis data is sent to `YOUR_DOMAIN` for AI processing.
We do not sell data to third parties.

## Screenshot handling

Screenshots are captured only when the user clicks Analyze.
Screenshots are sent to our server for visual AI analysis and are **not stored**; they are deleted after processing.

## Data retention

Audit results (URL, score, and pattern categories) are retained for 90 days and then automatically deleted.

## User rights

Users can delete their audit history via the dashboard.
The extension can be uninstalled at any time.

## Contact

`privacy@YOUR_DOMAIN`
